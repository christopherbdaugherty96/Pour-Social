POUR SOCIAL
Classic Event Cocktail Menu

Designed for: graduation parties, birthdays, backyard events, casual corporate gatherings.

This menu focuses on crowd-favorite cocktails that are fast to produce, recognizable to guests, and ideal for high-volume service.

Maximum menu size is intentionally limited to ensure quick service and consistent drink quality.

Service Notes

This menu works best for events where:
• guests prefer familiar cocktails
• speed of service is important
• event volume is moderate to high

Recommended for events of 40–120 guests.

# 1. Mobile Bar Setup (Core Equipment)

These are the **visual and functional centerpieces** of your setup.

## Portable Bar

You need **at least one portable bar station**.

Options:

* Folding bar
* Rustic wood bar
* Bar trailer
* Custom mobile bar

Typical size:

* **6–8 feet**

Typical cost:

| Item                        | Price            |
| --------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Portable folding bar        | $200 – $600      |
| Rustic wedding bar          | $500 – $1,500    |
| Bar trailer (later upgrade) | $3,000 – $10,000 |

Start with:

✔ **1 portable bar**

---

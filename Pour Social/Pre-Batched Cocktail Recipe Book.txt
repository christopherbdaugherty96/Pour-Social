POUR SOCIAL
Pre-Batched Cocktail Recipe Book
(Event Scale)

Purpose

Pre-batching cocktails significantly improves service speed during high-volume events.

The following recipes are scaled to produce approximately 40–50 drinks per batch depending on pour size.

---

Classic Margarita Batch

Ingredients

3 bottles tequila (750ml each)
1 bottle triple sec
32 oz fresh lime juice
16 oz simple syrup

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a large food-safe container.

Chill before service.

Serve over ice with salted rim.

Yield

Approximately 45 margaritas.

---

Cosmopolitan Batch

Ingredients

2 bottles vodka
1 bottle triple sec
32 oz cranberry juice
16 oz lime juice

Instructions

Combine ingredients in batching container.

Shake individual servings with ice before serving.

Yield

Approximately 40 cocktails.

---

Lavender Lemon Drop Batch

Ingredients

2 bottles vodka
24 oz lemon juice
16 oz lavender syrup
8 oz simple syrup

Instructions

Combine ingredients and chill.

Shake with ice before serving.

Sugar rim optional.

Yield

Approximately 40 cocktails.

---

Rum Punch Batch

Ingredients

2 bottles white rum
32 oz pineapple juice
16 oz orange juice
8 oz lime juice
8 oz simple syrup

Instructions

Combine ingredients in large container.

Serve over ice with citrus garnish.

Yield

Approximately 45 drinks.

---

Paloma Batch

Ingredients

2 bottles tequila
32 oz grapefruit juice
16 oz lime juice
12 oz simple syrup

Top with grapefruit soda when serving.

Yield

Approximately 40 drinks.

---

Sangria Batch

Ingredients

4 bottles wine
12 oz brandy
16 oz orange juice
8 oz simple syrup
Sliced citrus and berries

Instructions

Combine ingredients and refrigerate at least 6 hours before service.

Yield

Approximately 40 servings.

---

Batching Best Practices

• Always keep batches refrigerated before service
• Label each container clearly
• Shake or stir before pouring if separation occurs
• Prepare garnishes in advance for speed of service

---

Recommended Batch Cocktails for Events

Best cocktails to pre-batch:

Margaritas
Cosmopolitans
Rum Punch
Sangria
Palomas

These drinks maintain quality and allow bartenders to serve guests quickly.

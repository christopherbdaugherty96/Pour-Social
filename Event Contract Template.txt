2. Services Provided

Pour Social agrees to provide the following services for the event:

• Professional bartending staff
• Bar tools and equipment required for drink preparation
• Drink preparation and guest service
• Mixers and garnishes (if included in the selected package)
• Bar setup prior to service
• Bar breakdown and cleanup after service

---

3. Service Duration

Standard service includes up to four (4) hours of bar service unless otherwise specified in the event agreement.

Service duration begins at the scheduled bar service start time listed in this agreement.

---

4. Minimum Service Requirement

All service packages are based on a minimum contracted service period.

The full contracted service rate is required regardless of the actual duration of bar service.

If the event ends earlier than scheduled, or if the client or venue requests the bar to close prior to the contracted service end time, the full service fee remains due and payable.

The contracted service fee reserves bartending staff, preparation time, travel, setup, and equipment for the scheduled event period.

---

5. Additional Service Time

Additional service hours beyond the contracted service duration may be added at an agreed hourly rate.

Additional service time must be approved by both the client and Pour Social during the event.

Payment for additional hours must be made before the conclusion of service unless otherwise agreed.

---

6. Alcohol Policy

Pour Social operates as a mobile bartending service and does not sell alcohol.

Clients must choose one of the following options:

Option 1 — Client Supplies Alcohol

Client is responsible for purchasing and providing all alcohol for the event.

Option 2 — Client Pre-Orders Alcohol

Client may pre-order alcohol from a retailer and Pour Social may pick up the order up to 1–3 days prior to the event provided the order has been fully paid for by the client.

Pour Social only transports alcohol and does not purchase or sell alcohol.

---

7. Alcohol Quantity Responsibility

Pour Social may provide alcohol purchasing recommendations based on guest count and drink menu.

However, the client is responsible for ensuring sufficient alcohol is provided for the event.

Pour Social is not responsible for alcohol shortages during the event.

---

8. Responsible Alcohol Service

Pour Social bartenders reserve the right to:

• request identification when age is uncertain
• refuse service to minors
• refuse service to guests who appear intoxicated
• discontinue service if unsafe conditions arise

These decisions are made at the discretion of bartending staff to comply with responsible alcohol service standards.

---

9. Guest Count Changes

The client must notify Pour Social of significant guest count changes at least 7 days before the event.

Additional bartending staff may be required depending on final guest count.

Additional staffing costs may apply.

---

10. Venue Requirements

Client is responsible for confirming that alcohol service is permitted at the event venue.

Client must ensure that the venue provides:

• adequate space for bar setup
• reasonable access for setup and breakdown
• safe working conditions for bartending staff

---

11. Setup and Access

Pour Social requires access to the event location at least 60–90 minutes before scheduled service time in order to properly set up the bar area.

Delays caused by venue access restrictions may reduce actual service time without affecting the contracted service fee.

POUR SOCIAL
Mobile Bartending Service
Business Specifications — Current Operational Blueprint

Business Name
Pour Social

Location
Belleville, Michigan

Business Model

Pour Social operates as a mobile bartending service providing professional bartenders, drink preparation, bar equipment, mixers, and event bar setup.

Alcohol is not sold by Pour Social.

Alcohol Policy

Clients may choose one of the following options.

Option 1 — Client Supplies Alcohol

Clients purchase and bring all alcohol to the event.

Pour Social provides a recommended alcohol shopping list based on guest count and drink menu.

Option 2 — Client Pre-Orders Alcohol

Clients may pre-order alcohol from a retailer of their choice.

Pour Social may pick up the order on the client's behalf up to 1–3 days before the event provided the order has been fully paid for by the client.

Pour Social only transports alcohol and does not purchase or sell alcohol.

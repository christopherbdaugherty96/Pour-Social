POUR SOCIAL
Alcohol Shopping Calculator
(Client Planning Tool)

Purpose

The Pour Social Alcohol Calculator helps clients estimate how much alcohol they should purchase for their event.

Pour Social provides:

• mixers
• soda
• juices
• fruit and garnishes
• syrups
• bar tools and equipment

Clients only need to purchase the alcohol.

Average Consumption

The standard estimate used for events is:

1 drink per person per hour

Example:

100 guests
4 hour service

Estimated drinks served:

350–400 drinks

Important Note

Pour Social supplies all mixers, soda, fruit, and cocktail ingredients.

Clients only need to provide the alcohol based on the recommended shopping list.

---

Alcohol Purchase Options

Option 1

Client purchases alcohol and brings it to the event.

Option 2

Client pre-orders alcohol from a retailer.

Pour Social can pick up the order 2–3 days before the event once it has been fully paid for by the client.

Pour Social does not sell or purchase alcohol.

---

Helpful Tip

It is always recommended to purchase slightly more alcohol than the estimate to avoid running out during the event.

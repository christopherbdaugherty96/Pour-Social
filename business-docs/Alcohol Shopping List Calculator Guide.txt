POUR SOCIAL
Alcohol Shopping List Guide

Purpose

This guide helps clients determine how much alcohol to purchase for their event based on guest count, event duration, and drink menu selection.

Pour Social does not sell alcohol. Clients purchase alcohol themselves or pre-order it from a retailer.

General Rule

Most guests consume:

1 drink per hour.

Example:

4 hour event
100 guests

Average drinks served: 350–400 drinks.

Drink Type Breakdown (Typical Event)

Beer: 40%
Wine: 30%
Cocktails: 30%

If the event focuses heavily on cocktails, the cocktail percentage may increase.

Mixer Quantities

For most events the following mixers are recommended:

Club soda — 12–18 cans
Tonic water — 12 cans
Ginger beer — 12–18 bottles
Cranberry juice — 2–3 large bottles
Pineapple juice — 2 large bottles
Orange juice — 2 large bottles
Lime juice — 1 large bottle

Garnishes

Limes — 25–40
Lemons — 15–25
Mint — 3–4 bunches
Orange slices — 6–8 oranges

Ice

Plan for approximately:

1–1.5 pounds of ice per guest.

Example:

100 guests → 100–150 pounds ice.

Event Planning Tip

Limiting cocktail menus to 6–8 drinks improves service speed and reduces alcohol costs.

Pour Social can help adjust alcohol quantities based on your final drink menu.

Alcohol Pickup Option

Clients may also pre-order alcohol from a retailer and request Pour Social pickup up to 1–3 days before the event.

Orders must be fully paid before pickup.

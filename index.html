About Pour Social

Pour Social is an event bar staffing & beverage experience company serving weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations.

We focus on planning, staffing coordination, menus, and quality control so clients get a polished experience without handling the bar themselves.

Pour Social does not sell alcohol. Clients provide or pre-order alcohol while we coordinate service, mixers, garnishes, tools, and execution.

Based in Belleville, Michigan, serving Southeast Michigan.

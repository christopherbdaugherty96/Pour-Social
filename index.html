About Pour Social

Pour Social is an event bar staffing & beverage experience company focused on organized, polished execution rather than one person bartending every event.

The company coordinates staffing, beverage planning, service standards, mixers, garnishes, and execution so clients can book one brand instead of piecing the experience together themselves.

Clients provide or pre-order alcohol. Pour Social does not sell alcohol and is designed around planning, coordination, staffing, and event quality control.

Based in Belleville, Michigan, with service focus across Southeast Michigan including Ann Arbor, Detroit, Canton, Plymouth, Ypsilanti, Novi, and Livonia.

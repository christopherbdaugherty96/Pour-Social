About Pour Social

Pour Social is a mobile bartending service focused on elevating events through polished service, handcrafted cocktails, and exceptional hospitality.

With over a decade of experience across fine dining, nightlife, and private events, our team creates custom bar experiences for weddings, corporate gatherings, and celebrations of all sizes.

Pour Social does not sell alcohol. Clients provide or pre-order alcohol while we provide staffing, tools, mixers, fruit, garnishes, and execution.

Based in Belleville, Michigan, we serve Southeast Michigan including Ann Arbor, Detroit, Canton, Plymouth, Ypsilanti, Novi, and Livonia.

FAQ

What are your starting package prices?
Basic starts at $450, Signature starts at $750, and Premium/Wedding starts at $1,200+. Final pricing depends on guest count, staffing, hours, setup, and location.

Do you provide mixers and garnishes?
Yes. Pour Social provides mixers, juices, syrups, fruit, garnishes, and bar tools.

Do you sell alcohol?
No. Clients provide or pre-order alcohol, and we provide full bar service.

Can you help us plan menu and quantities?
Absolutely. Use the pricing and alcohol tools, or ask the AI bartender for a quick planning start.

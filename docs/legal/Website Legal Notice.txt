POUR SOCIAL
Alcohol Service Policy

Pour Social provides professional mobile bartending services for private events.

Pour Social does not sell alcohol.

Clients are responsible for purchasing and providing all alcohol served at their event.

Alcohol Options

Clients may choose one of the following options:

Client Supplies Alcohol

Clients purchase and bring all alcohol to the event.
Pour Social can provide a recommended alcohol shopping list based on guest count and drink menu.

Client Pre-Orders Alcohol

Clients may pre-order alcohol from a retailer of their choice.

Pour Social may pick up the order on the client's behalf up to 1–3 days before the event provided the order has been fully paid for by the client.

In this case, Pour Social only transports alcohol and does not purchase or sell alcohol.

Responsible Service

Pour Social bartenders practice responsible alcohol service and reserve the right to refuse service to any guest who appears intoxicated or cannot provide valid proof of legal drinking age.

Legal Drinking Age

All guests consuming alcohol must be 21 years of age or older.

Venue Requirements

Clients must ensure that their chosen venue allows alcohol service and complies with local regulations.

Questions

For additional questions regarding alcohol service policies please contact Pour Social before booking your event.
